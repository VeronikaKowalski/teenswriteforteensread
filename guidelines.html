Rules & Guidelines

Consider the following when sending in writing:




No vulgar or inappropriate language. We cannot post work that has inappropriate content. If a story contains such content, it will be edited so as not to affect the meaning of the piece. If writing cannot be edited while preserving the essence of the story, the work will be discarded.



Do not submit anyone else's work under your name, or your work under anyone else's name. Plagiarism is a crime.



No spamming. This one isn't illegal, but just don't be a jerk.



Have fun!
